Our Story

LukouHub started with a simple idea after discovering Greek donuts during a trip to Australia. We wanted to bring this delightful experience to Borneo!

Since 2022, we've been crafting authentic Greek donuts and bomboloni with love, using quality ingredients to create memorable sweet moments for our community in Kuching.

From our humble beginnings at Gala City to now operating two outlets, we're grateful for every customer who has been part of our journey.
