## About

LukouHub is a web-based e-commerce platform designed to showcase and sell authentic Greek donuts and bomboloni. The website provides customers with an intuitive shopping experience, featuring various package options from solo treats to party boxes.

### Key Highlights:
- Authentic Greek loukoumades recipes
- Handcrafted bomboloni with Malaysian twist
- Two locations in Kuching (Gala City Mall & Vivacity Megamall)
- Full shopping cart functionality with promo codes
- Fully responsive design for all devices

## Features
- **Product Catalog**: Browse through various donut packages (Solo, Couple, Family, Party)
- **Shopping Cart**: Add, remove, and modify product quantities
- **Promo Codes**: Apply discount codes at checkout
- **Responsive Design**: Seamless experience on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Product Filtering**: Filter products by category
- **Search Functionality**: Quickly find your favorite donuts

## Usage

### For Customers

1. **Browse Products**: Visit the home page to explore featured items
2. **View Menu**: Click "Menu" to see all available products
3. **Add to Cart**: Click the "+" button on any product
4. **Manage Cart**: 
   - Adjust quantities with +/- buttons
   - Remove items with the trash icon
   - Apply promo codes for discounts
5. **Checkout**: Click "Proceed to Checkout" when ready
